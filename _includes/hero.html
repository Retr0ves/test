D.O.C.C. Lab is a research group at Tufts University that focuses on the diagnosis, observability, and configuration of cloud systems.

Our varied research explores: Automated debugging tools, Engineer-assisting tools and visualizations, User-friendly problem-solving tools , Addressing security and privacy issues in diagnosing complex system problems, Investigating usability aspects of diagnosis tools , and Developing principled distributed systems for enhanced debugging.
